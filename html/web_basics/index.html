Indian Smoothie

An Indian smoothie is a bit like a gazpacho. It is a cooked dish blended well and turned into a smoothie or a cold soup. It's an excellent way to get lots of nutrients on a daily basis.

Here is a list of ingredients for a typical Indian smoothie.

  • kale
  • tomatoes
  • onion
  • green chilies
  • fresh ginger
  • turmeric
  • black pepper
  • mustard seeds
  • cumin seeds
  • salt
  • Canola oil

Here are the steps to make this amazing smoothie.

  1. Heat 1/4 c oil in a large pan using medium heat.
  2. Add 1/2 t cumin seed and 1/2 t mustard seeds. Heat until the mustard seeds pop.
  3. Add 1/2 c diced onions. Stir frequently. Cook well.
  4. Add 1 T minced ginger and 2 diced green chilies. Stir for several minutes.
  5. Add 5 small diced tomatoes. Stir occassionally until tomatoes become a paste.
  6. Add 1 c water.
  7. Add 3 bunches of chopped kale. Stir occassionally until kale cooks down.
  8. Add 1 t turmeric and 1/4 t black pepper.
  9. Add 1/2 t salt. Stir occasionally.
  10. Remove from heat when kale is cooked to your preference.
  11. Fill Blentec or Vitamix 1/3 full of kale mixture and add water to more than cover the mixture. Blend well. Repeat final step as more smoothies are needed.
